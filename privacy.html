Privacy Policy

History Digest Chrome Extension · Last updated: May 2026

What data we access

History Digest reads your Chrome browsing history for the date you select. This requires the history permission.

What we do with it

  • All processing happens entirely on your device, inside the extension.
  • No browsing data is ever sent to any server, third party, or external service.
  • The only output is a Markdown or JSON file written to your local ~/Downloads folder.

What we store

The extension saves two preferences to Chrome's local storage (on your device only): your chosen output folder and your preferred export format. No browsing history is stored by the extension.

Permissions used

  • history — to read your browsing history for the selected date.
  • downloads — to write the exported file to your Downloads folder.
  • storage — to persist your folder and format preferences locally.
